The James Webb Space Telescope

Much of the history of our universe remains a mystery to us. The capabilities of the James Webb Space Telescope will drive humanity further into our understanding of the cosmos by looking billions of years into the past at the earliest galaxies, as well as observing exoplanets that humanity may one day step foot on.
